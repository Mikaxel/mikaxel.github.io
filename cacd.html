Summary

Concept Art & Character Design in a course at JAMK University of Applied Sciences. In this course I have learnt quite a lot about things, that help you to develop your characters. This course has also given me a chance to improve my concept art skills.

Softwares and tools

I mostly used softwares that were made by Affinity. These softwares are Affinity Designer and Affinity Photo. Designer is for vector art and Photo is for raster graphics.
